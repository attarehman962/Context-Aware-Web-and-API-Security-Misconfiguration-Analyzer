Workflow

How CAWASMA Works

CAWASMA follows a focused analysis cycle: launch a scan, discover endpoints, evaluate misconfigurations, prioritize findings, and review the resulting report.

1. Enter Target

Submit the target web application or API endpoint that CAWASMA should inspect.

2. Discover and Analyze

The crawler maps reachable routes and the analysis engine evaluates headers, responses, cookies, and exposure signals.

3. Findings Are Prioritized

Context-aware scoring adjusts severity and correlates related signals into actionable findings and exploit chains.

4. Review Report

Inspect scan summaries, evidence, risk scores, and correlated chains through the reporting interface.
